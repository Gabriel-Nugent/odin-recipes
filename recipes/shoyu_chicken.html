Shoyu Chicken

Juicy shoyu chicken topped with green onions

This shoyu chicken is a really easy and quick weeknight meal that contains all the umami that shoyu can offer.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken Stock
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to a large pot
  2. Bring the pot to a boil
  3. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes
  4. Remove chicken from the pot and thicken to serve over rice
