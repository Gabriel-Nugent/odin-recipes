Fried Rice

A personal all-time favorite.

You can never go wrong with fried rice. There's something about the savory grains interlaced with crunchy vegetables that is absolutely irresistable.

Ingredients

  • Protein of choice
  • Day-old cooked rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Ketchup
  • Frozen vegetables of your choosing
  • cooking oil
  • egg

Steps

  1. Scramble the egg in a hot saucepan with oil
  2. Add your protein source and cook thoroughly
  3. Add the frozen vegetables and cook until tender
  4. Add the cooked rice with all the spices and sauces
  5. Cook until all the rice has been heated and coated in sauce
