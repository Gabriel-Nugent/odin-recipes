Spaghetti

This classic never disapoints.

Simple, easy, and always delicious, spaghetti is a recipe that all home cooks should know by heart

Ingredients

  • Dry spaghetti noodles
  • Jarred Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic and add it to a saucepan with the oil
  2. Once the garlic has turned golden brown, add in the pasta sauce
  3. Set the heat to low and simmer until the sauce has thickened
  4. Meanwhile, add the pasta to a large pot of boiling water
  5. Once both the sauce and pasta are finished, combine them and serve
